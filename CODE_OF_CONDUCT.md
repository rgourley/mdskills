# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community and the project
- Helping others learn and contribute

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to the project

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please email the maintainer directly or open a private report through GitHub.
